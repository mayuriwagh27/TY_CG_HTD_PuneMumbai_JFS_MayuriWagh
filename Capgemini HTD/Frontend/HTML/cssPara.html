Wikipedia is a multilingual online encyclopedia created and maintained as an open collaboration project by a community
of volunteer editors using a wiki-based editing system. It is the largest and most popular general reference
work on the World Wide Web, and is one of the most popular websites ranked by Alexa as of October 2019.
